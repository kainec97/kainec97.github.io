About Me

General Information

My name is Kaine Cochrane. I am currently 19 years old and was born on the 11th of October, 1997. At the moment I am living independently in South Brisbane, and planning to complete my studies through distance education (at Southern Cross University) by February 2018.

I graduated from Elanora State High School at the end of 2014. During my final year of study there, I received direct entry into Southern Cross University, and started my Bachelor of IT in early 2015.

Hobbies and Interests

My main interest is IT (such as dealing with computer hardware, software and video games) although I have an interest in music (both in terms of recreation and production, although the latter I have not delved into too deeply as of yet) and art (although I have very limited experience in drawing but wish to pursue it as a hobby sometime soon) as well. I’ve been interested in IT for practically my whole life, using computers since I was around 5 years old and recently building a computer for a friend and my mother (both of which are still running well). My interest in videos games stems from the fact I have been playing them from a young age, and dabbled in some basic video game development both at school and at home (as well as some 3D modelling).

Plans for the Future

So, as you may expect, I’m striving to find a career in IT at some point in my life (hopefully not long after I graduate from University). I have completed my first and (almost) second year, and expecting to complete the entire three year course. I'm hoping to achieve a career in IT by:

Finding a part-time job that relates to IT in some way (although not compulsory it would assist greatly),
completing the Bachelor of IT course successfully and achieving high grades, and
searching for full-time employment in the field not long after graduating.

At the time of writing (December 2016), the career choice I am most invested in is web design/development. While this isn't a concrete decision, it's the one I am currently most confident with.
