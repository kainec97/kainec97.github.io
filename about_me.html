About Me

General Information

My name is Kaine Cochrane. I am currently 20 years old and was born on the 11th of October, 1997. At the moment I am living in south Brisbane (Calamvale), and have recently completed my studies at Southern Cross University. I'll be graduating in March of 2018.

I graduated from Elanora State High School at the end of 2014. During my final year of study there, I received direct entry into Southern Cross University via the STAR Entry Scheme, and started my Bachelor of IT in early 2015.


Hobbies and Interests

My main interest is IT (computer hardware, software and video games). I also have an interest in music (in terms of recreation and production, although the latter I have not delved into too deeply as of yet). I’ve been interested in IT for practically my whole life, using computers since I was around 5 years old and recently building a computer for a friend and my mother. My interest in videos games stems from the fact I have been playing them from a young age, and attempted some basic video game development both at school and at home (as well as some 3D modelling in Blender).
